Measuring Vertical Stiffness and Damping of Bicycle Tires

  • Sat 17 January 2015
  • research
  • Arend L. Schwab
  • #bicycle, #tire, #schwab blog

A group of TU Delft BSc students (Jibbe Knuit, Frans Kok, Philip Raaphorst and Arne Spek) did a project on measuring vertical stiffness and damping of bicycle tires. A total of 11 tires were measured at 3 normal loads and 3 internal pressures. The setup, data and results can be found at this website: http://bicycle.tudelft.nl/tiretestingvertical/
