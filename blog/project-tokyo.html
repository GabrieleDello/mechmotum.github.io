Project Tokyo

  • Thu 07 March 2019
  • news
  • Arend L. Schwab
  • #schwab blog, #olympics

Project Tokyo; the new track bicycle for the Dutch Olympic Track cycling team has been presented. This new track bicycle, the KOGA KINSEI, has been designed and build by a multidisplinary team, project Tokyo, with the partners:

KOGA - The project leaders, and bicycle builders
TU Delft - Bikefit, handling and control
Actiflow - Aerodynamic design
Pontis Engineering - Structural design

Each track bicycle is build individually for each rider such that he has maximum power output and good handling at minimal air drag and maximum structural integrity. More info (in Dutch) at: https://www.projecttokyo.nl/ and at https://www.koga.com/nl/baanfiets/over-de-koga-kinsei.htm
